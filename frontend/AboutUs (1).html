About ExpireMart

Reducing Waste • Saving Money • Smart Reselling

Who We Are

ExpireMart is an innovative marketplace that helps reduce wastage of near-expiry products by enabling people and businesses to resell sealed products at discounted prices.

Our platform connects sellers, inspection agents, buyers and delivery partners to build a transparent ecosystem for verified resale.

Our Mission

Millions of products are thrown away every year simply because they are close to expiry. ExpireMart solves this by creating a trusted marketplace where these products can still be safely used and sold at lower prices.

How ExpireMart Works

1. Sellers

List sealed near-expiry products with images and expiry details.

2. Inspection

Inspection agents verify product condition and expiry validity.

3. Buyers

Buyers purchase verified products at discounted prices.

4. Delivery

Delivery partners safely deliver products to buyers.

Terms & Conditions

  • All products must be sealed and unused.
  • Products must have more than one month before expiry.
  • Only OTC medicines are allowed on the platform.
  • All products must pass inspection before listing.
  • Inspection fee may be non-refundable if seller provides false information.
  • Final product price is decided after inspection.
  • ExpireMart reserves the right to reject any product violating policies.
