ExpiryMart

Smart Marketplace for Near-Expiry Essentials

ExpiryMart is a peer-to-peer digital marketplace designed to reduce the wastage of near-expiry essential products such as medicines, FMCG goods, and personal care items. Many usable products are discarded simply because they are approaching their expiry date, resulting in financial loss and unnecessary waste.

ExpiryMart provides a secure platform where verified sellers can list sealed products that are close to expiry. Before being made available to buyers, each product is physically inspected and verified to ensure safety, authenticity, and compliance with platform rules.

The system connects sellers, inspection agents, buyers, and delivery partners through a role-based workflow that enables safe resale, fair pricing, and location-based delivery. By combining product verification, controlled pricing models, and inspection processes, ExpiryMart aims to promote sustainable consumption while making essential goods more affordable for everyone.
